LASTBITE AI 

PROBLEM STATEMENT
Food waste is one of the most overlooked environmental crises of our time. Globally, 1 in 3 meals worth of food never gets eaten — and the majority of that waste starts right at home, inside ordinary refrigerators. The problem isn't that people don't care. It's that when you open your  and see half a wilting spinach bunch, two eggs, some leftover rice, and milk that expires tomorrow — your brain freezes. You can't think of what to make. You order Zomato instead. Three days later you throw everything out. This happens in millions of households every single day. The food rots. The money is gone. And the environmental cost is staggering — food waste alone accounts for 8-10% of global greenhouse gas emissions, more than the entire aviation industry combined. Yet there is no tool that looks at YOUR specific dying ingredients, on YOUR specific timeline, and tells you exactly what to cook before it's too late. Recipe apps show you meals but don't know what's expiring. Meal planners ask you to shop for new ingredients. Nothing bridges the gap between what's already in your and a plan to actually use it. That gap is what we're solving.
THE SOLUTION
LastBite AI is a zero-waste kitchen intelligence app. You open it, tap the ingredients currently expiring, and tag each one with how many days it has left. Our AI — powered by Claude — instantly analyses your exact inventory, prioritises the ingredients closest to expiry, and generates a complete personalised 7-day meal plan with breakfast, lunch, and dinner for every day. Every meal is built around what you already have. The most urgent ingredients appear in Day 1 and Day 2 meals first, so nothing expires before it gets used. The app shows you a Waste Rescue Score — the percentage of your expiring ingredients the plan successfully uses up — and calculates the exact CO₂ emissions you prevented by not throwing that food away. It also generates a minimal top-up shopping list: the 3 to 5 cheap bridge ingredients you might need to complete the week. No account needed, no lengthy onboarding, no asking you to buy anything new. Just tap what's dying, get your plan in under 10 seconds, and cook with confidence.
BRIEF IDEA
LastBite AI is the app that turns your dying  into a week of delicious meals. Most food waste happens because people , don't know what to cook with what they see, and give up. We fix that with AI. You tap your expiring ingredients, we build your entire week's meal plan around them — prioritising the most urgent items so nothing goes bad before it gets eaten. You see your waste rescue score, your CO₂ saved, and a shopping list for anything you're missing. The whole experience takes under a minute. It's not a recipe app. It's not a meal planner. It's a food waste eliminator disguised as a personal chef — and it lives entirely in your browser.
